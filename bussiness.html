Our Bussiness

M.K Production

M.K Production, a venture under the Mukkukadayil Group, is a leading manufacturer of high-quality beverages in Kerala. Established with a commitment to providing pure and refreshing products to its customers, M.K Production has grown to become a trusted name in the beverage industry.
We specialize in the manufacturing of soft drinks, juices, club soda, and packaged drinking water in various sizes, catering to a wide range of consumer preferences. Our range of products includes popular brands such as Shudh Life, Licit, Daily New, and Pam Neer.
At M.K Production, we use state-of-the-art technology and follow strict quality control measures to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and purity. Our manufacturing unit is equipped with the latest machinery, which helps us to achieve maximum production capacity and maintain consistency in taste and quality across all our products.
We strive to provide our customers with a delightful and refreshing experience with every sip of our products. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has been the driving force behind our growth and success in the industry.

Hira Marketing

Hira Marketing is another subsidiary of Mukkukadayil Group and is responsible for marketing 20-liter jars for MK Production. The company was established with the goal of providing innovative and effective marketing solutions to support the growth of Mukkukadayil Group's beverage operations. With a team of experienced marketers and a commitment to excellence, Hira Marketing helps to ensure the success and visibility of Mukkukadayil Group's brands in the market.
