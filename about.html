About Us



Mukkukadayil Group is a dynamic and innovative company with a strong presence in the beverage industry. Our founder, who has over 25 years of experience in the beverage industry, has brought his expertise and knowledge to the market with his own company.
At Mukkukadayil Group, we are home to several successful ventures in the beverage industry, including M.K Production, M.K. General Traders, Hira Marketing, and Green Marketing. Our businesses manufacture and distribute a range of products such as soft drinks, juices, club soda, and packaged drinking water.
Under our various brands, we offer a diverse range of products to cater to different consumer preferences and needs. Our brands include Shudh Life, Licit, Daily New, and Pam Neer, each focused on specific product categories and quantities.
We place a specific focus on the quality of our drinking water products, with premium offerings under the Shudh Life and Licit brands, and a new premium drinking water brand, Pam Neer, on the horizon. Our other brands, Daily New, handle the categories of soft drinks, juice, and club soda, offering a variety of flavours and quantities to consumers.
Mukkukadayil Group has established itself as a leader in the beverage industry, with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our success is driven by our team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to producing high-quality products and providing exceptional customer service.
Incorporating the latest technologies and manufacturing processes, we strive to continuously improve our products and services to meet the ever-evolving needs of our customers. Our ultimate goal is to provide outstanding choices for families to have a magnificent shopping experience with a variety of brands at reasonable prices under one roof.
Mukkukadayil Group has a bright future ahead, with plans to expand our range of products and extend our presence to new markets. We are committed to making a positive impact on the communities we serve and are excited to continue our growth and success in the years to come.
